About Me

This enabled me learn about not just spanish but the latin base in which so many languages are derived and grew an understanding and appreciation of other cultures. For years I supported our family doing food service jobs. I worked at restataunts, did pizza delivery, and even became a general manager. During that time my wife learned web development skills, where she was able to get an amazing job doing what she enjoys. Now it's my turn to use my strengths of language, logic, math, and communication to persue my interests in software development, building automations, and fixing bugs.
